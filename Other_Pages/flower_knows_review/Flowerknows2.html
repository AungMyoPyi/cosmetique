More about Flower Knows

Flower Knows, a Chinese makeup brand that emerged in 2016, dedicated to crafting fairy tale-inspired cosmetics and fulfilling the dreams of all women with a girlish heart. Embracing the belief that the pursuit of beauty is a journey of self-healing, Flower Knows meticulously designs and produces its cosmetics with utmost care. Its rapid expansion across Southeast Asia, Japan, Europe, and the US is a testament to its popularity.
