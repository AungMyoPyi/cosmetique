About Rare Beauty

Rare Beauty is a cosmetics brand founded by the multi-talented artist, singer, and actress Selena Gomez. Launched in September 2020, Rare Beauty quickly made waves in the beauty industry with its mission to celebrate individuality, foster self-expression, and promote inclusivity. The brand's name, "Rare," encapsulates its core values of embracing uniqueness and authenticity. Rare Beauty's product lineup includes a diverse range of makeup essentials, from foundations and concealers to lipsticks and eyeshadows. One of the brand's signature products is its Liquid Touch Weightless Foundation.In addition to its product offerings, Rare Beauty has garnered attention for its sleek and sustainable packaging. The brand prioritizes eco-conscious practices, using recycled materials whenever possible and incorporating refillable components into its packaging designs.
